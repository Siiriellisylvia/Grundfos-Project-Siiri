In the News section you can find short announcements about the dormitory's affairs.
  • You can choose a category using the filters.
In the Events section you can find upcoming parties and workshops. There are highlighted events and calendar to give you an overview of the current and approaching month.
  • Click the calendar to see all events
  • Send an event suggestion through the button on the bottom of the page
In the Laundry section you can check the live-status of washing machines and tumblers. The line graph can help you improve your chances of encountering free washing machine!
  • Data is updated on weekly basis based on real user statistics.
  • You're welcome, Tobias!
In the Contact form section you can send a message to your Caretaker or to the Resident Council.
  • Remember to choose the subject!
  • This section requires log in using your chip or e-mail and password to your Resident Account.
In the Facilities section you can see where everything is located in the building.
  • To highlight a facility on the building layout, simply tap the floor number.
In the Poll of the Week section, every week you can answer one question related to tons of different, funny subjects. Let the dormitory's community know what your opinions or preferences are!
  • Send a poll suggestion through the button on the bottom of the page
  • You can also see results of the last week's poll!
